ALL ABOUT GHIBLI

PELICULAS ANIMADAS

Studio Ghibli, Inc. (スタジオジブリ), is a Japanese animation film studio based in Koganei, Tokyo. They were founded by Isao Takahata, Hayao Miyazaki, Toshio Suzuki and Yasuyoshi Tokuma. The studio is best known for its animated feature films, and has also produced several short subjects, TV commercials and a TV movie. It has also collaborated with video game studios on the visual development of several video games. Five of the studio's films are among the top ten highest-grossing anime films made in Japan. Spirited Away is second, grossing ¥31.68 billion in Japan and over $380 million worldwide, and Princess Mononoke is fourth, grossing ¥20.18 billion.
